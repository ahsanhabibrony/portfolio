Looking for a Fully Flexible , Asynchronous Online Education Experience With Fully Accredited Courses From One of the [tin_keyword] Providers in the Country While Building Your Values With a Christian World View?

Why Choose Westminster Christian Online?

  • Freedom to Learn– Asynchronous, fully flexible learning plans with courses that can be completed in as few as six weeks or as long as 18 months.

  • Flexibility – The flexibility to do school when you can on a schedule that matches your travel, health, and sleep patterns.

  • Helping You Get Started –Our Admissions staff will make the transition into online education as smooth as possible. We’re with you every step of the way.

  • Proven and Recognized Education –Fully accredited courses from one of the best, most experienced online course providers in the country. (most highly-rated Christian schools in the country).

  • Christian Worldview First – Authentic Christian worldview built into to class assignments, readings, discussions, in all subject areas.

  • Build For Future Education –One-hundred percent of Westminster students continue their education at four year universities and colleges, including some of the nation’s top schools. Advanced Placement and Dual Credit offerings that can help you earn credit for college.

  • Athletic Minded – NCAA approved courses so athletes can continue your college eligibility path.

  • Your School, Your Experience –Expert college counseling from a seasoned team that makes college counseling personal and specific, not a one size fits all approach many schools take with overloaded school counselors.

  • No Commute –No time wasted with tortuous commutes to the far away Christian school.

The mission of Westminster Christian School is to empower learners, prepare hearts, and inspire lives to faithfully serve as ambassadors for Jesus Christ. Westminster Online allows us to extend our mission and offer our premium, Christ-centered education beyond South Florida to Christian families who value a Christian education from a biblical worldview.

How Does It Work?

  • You apply to Westminster’s online program as you would to any private school, with your school transcripts, teacher/pastor recommendations, and take a test (Independent Schools Entrance Examination-ISSE through the Educational Records Bureau).
  • Once admitted, you will be on pace for a Westminster Christian Online diploma. You take a full complement of core classes just like the students at Westminster’s Palmetto Bay campus, but you have some flexibility on your electives.
  • You will receive a Microsoft Surface Pro to connect seamlessly with your online curriculum.
  • Expert college counseling from seasoned professionals who will have the time to video conference with you and your family to help you on your college journey

How To Get Started

1. Submit an online application, including application fee.


2. Once your application has been processed, a member of our Admission Office will contact you and provide you access to a digital checklist of required documents


3. Once all documents have been submitted, and Admission testing is complete, you will be contacted to schedule a Family Interview
