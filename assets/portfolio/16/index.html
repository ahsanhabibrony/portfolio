8 Reasons

To let us feed you

01

Eat More Healthy

Every Farmbox is filled with produce that was harvested within 48-72 hours of your delivery. This means that your produce will be more fresh, nutritious, and tasty compared to your average grocery store.

02

Community connection

You have the chance to be an active part of Carolina's farming community while also getting to know your farmers through our Farm News, events, and social media.

03

pesticide free

Every farmer and artisan supplying Farmbox Carolina has pledged to abide by our pesticide-free guarantee. 100% of the produce we deliver is pesticide-free.

04

no commitment required

No complicated contracts. You can cancel or suspend your deliveries simply by contacting us before your customization period closes.

05

Flexibility

Choose (& change) the size and type of box that works for you. You can even skip deliveries or change your delivery frequency to fit your schedule.

06

customization

Use your never send list to exclude items you do not want. Add and/or swap out fruits and veggies. Add other farm products like eggs, dairy, jams and honey.

07

variety & selection

We bring the season's best mix of 100% produce and hand carfted farm products to your door by growing and partnering with local farms and astisans.

08

convenience

Save time going to the grocery store or remembering to pick up your produce at a specific time and location. Delivery to your door is included!
